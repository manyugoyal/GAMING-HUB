Services

International services with no boundaries

Inlanefreight delivers customized global freight solutions to meet your most demanding requirements for on-time performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. Our reach is large—more than 190 countries—thanks to a worldwide network of overseas partners, but we remain flexible enough to provide tailor-made shipping solutions that fit your exact scheduling and budget requirements. Plus, more than 45 years of experience in international shipping ensures accurate documentation, complete customs brokerage service, and expert knowledge of import/export requirements, regulations, licenses, and payment practices.

No matter how urgent, no matter from which location. With our express shipping offerings, you can secure your global supply chains for the long term. We can always find the right shipping solution to ensure that your pallets, packages, and documents get from A to B with confidence.

With us, you can handle your entire shipping process efficiently. Thanks to our price-performance overview, you can see all the shipping service providers’ costs and services at a glance. You can then select the best offer and send your packages, pallets, and documents directly: express, standard, superior, air freight, and courier.

Inlanefreight offers a unique range of functions to harmonize decentralized shipping processes, reduce costs, and save time. Shipping processing directly via the web application or the web service API (InlaneConnect) for integration into an existing IT structure.

Additional functions such as proactive tracking, online shipping archive, business intelligence reporting, and customs processing, combined with personal customer service, offer a unique coverage of the shipping needs of professional shippers for great flexibility and independence with full cost transparency.

More than 40 motivated logistics and software specialists work at seven international locations for Inlanefreight, a logistics company founded in 2020. Thanks to constant and continuous progress, Inlanefreight is the European market leader in this field.
